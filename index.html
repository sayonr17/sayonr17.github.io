About Us

I love coding!

Hey everyone! I'm Anweshan Roy Chowdhury. I'm a student of the University of Calcutta, studying Computer Science. I am about to graduate with a B.Sc (or B.S.) Honours Degree from the University.I finished schooling from La Martinere for Boys, gave my ICSE and ISC examinations, and finally enrolled myself for higher education. 

You may have fairly understood that I love coding. My interests are mainly on the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Backend Web Development, Front End Development, Automation on Embedded Systems.

Over the years I have fairly learnt many programming languages, some of which were self taught, while others were in the course. I was introduced to coding early in my life with a language called LOGO in standard 1 (maybe). From there it transitioned to BASIC from standard 6 through 8. From there onwards, I chose my 6th subject as "Computer Application" in class 9, with JAVA as the dominant language till standard 12.

I am fairly comfortable to write code in Python, C/C++, Kothlin, Shell/ Bash to name a few.

Lets start with the lowest machine language, i.e. Assembly Language (coding with 8085). Well I love the idea, but I do hate the attrocies amount of syntax and instructions needed.

Taking a step up, a quick mention to C/C++ & C#. I admire these languages the sole reason being the package it compiles itself is container native to the OS. This helps saving time to repackage a higher programming language to .exe/.dmg files. I am quite familier with Shell Programming.

In the same category, I would give a special mention to Prolog which I hate. I love SQL and is most familier with MySQL to PostgreSQL.

Most part of my coding career, I learnt how to think everything on the basis of JAVA. Still today if I am stuck, I write the pseudocode in JAVA, and then ultimately translate it into the required language.

As the Android market share is dominant & growing, I had to keep myself habituated with the JAVA syntax, when I learning a little bit of Kothlin.

With my interests in Machine Learning & Artificial Intelligence, you would be introduced my all time preference Python. I can only describe it as “Superb and Supreme”

Focusing on web development. The first part I would talk about is the Front End. Well I use html, and style my pages with CSS. I keep the intensity of front end Javascript dependency as low as possible. Even if I have most features toggled with Javascript, I still try to keep the page working without the support of the same.

As I mentioned CSS & responsive, I would quickly mention Bootstrap. Well a mention to a modified JS library jQuery which I have used extensively and my fallback from fancy javascript libraries.

Taking about simplicity, I would mention Markdown which is actually very useful in most cases.

I will just give some honorary metions to JSON, Git, SSH, Composer, npm, bower, YAML, Maven, Gradle.
